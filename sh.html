Carbon Rods

Carbon Rods have many industrial uses but less household applications which makes it ough to use for common people. But there are still some ideas to use this.

The first usecase is as a less efficient pencil. This is because carbon rods use the same material that makes the pencil right which is graphite, Although in carbon rods it is way stronger and also has way bigger particle sizes compared to pencil lead which makes it rougher.

Another usecase for carbon rods is to grind it down to a powder(Do this outside and with a mask as this is the most purest form of carbon and breathin it wouldnt be prety good) and then with this grinded down powder you can put it into any mechanism like a drawer roller to make it more smoother and better. This works as graphite acts as a self lubricating susbstance which allows it to make mechanisms smoother and less harsh.

Zinc Shelling

Zinc also like carbon rods have many industrial usecase but less of household uses. Although there is still stuff you can do with zinc.

One thing you can do with zinc is remelt it into a bigger shape like a cube and then break it open to show its beautiful blue crystals inside.

Other than that a more usefull usecase of zinc is in its antimicrobial property amon other things. And the way we use this is by makng something that may leverage that such as a spoon that is galvanised so that its easier to wash, or instead of using lead foil to help with cracks in your tiling, you can use zinc.This is usefull and healthy as zinc is a little less malleable than lead but instead of lead particles leachinbg into our water, its zinc whcih is a mineral that we need in our body.

Steel Casing

Out of all of the materials you can get from a battery, a steel casing has so many possibilities. And this is because the steel casing is made out fo stainless steel which stops it from rusting and negatively affecting whatever's inside.

One of these useceses is as a storage system. This is nice as if you add a cap on it, it will allow it to be used for storage at basically every angle. In the casig you can put anything you want. From cash, pills, water, small bits of food, or anything else you want yu can put inside of it, and most importantly because its cylindrical you can layer it ontop of another.

Another good use for this is in fishing. If you have some srap lead you can melt it into the casing and then drill a hole into it to pas a line through to make a damn cheap sinker, moreover you can get lead for basically free when you go into any scrapyard or abandonned lots as they're almost always there.
