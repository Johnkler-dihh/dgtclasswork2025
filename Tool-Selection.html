Tool Selection

Marksman Pliers

Marksman pliers by far are one of the best tools.This is because they consist of 3 very important parts of 3 different pliers, the first is a wire cutter, a gripper and a circular grip.

The main reason why i say this is on of the best tools is that even in a household situation a marksman plier saves space, costs and moreover is more time efficient in different senarios.But most importantly is almost perfect for our usecase

Marksman NosePliers

The marksman Nose Pliers are the most perfect tool anybody could have. This is because eventhough it seems basiclaly the same the main diffference is that it has a very narrow gripper tip whch allows for more intricate and clean operations.

Moreover, this is The Tool for the job.This is because the narrow tip alows us to grab onto the graphite rod and tear apart the zinc ery easiyl without damagin it , whilst wth the regular marksman pliers it usually ends up flattening the zinc and crushes up the graphite.
